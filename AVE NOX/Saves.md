# Saves

| Save                       | Description                                                                      | alternatives                    | alt. desc.                                                    |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Death or Poison            | When targeted by a death ray or exposed to poison                                | Save v. Poison                  | Poisons and disease                                           |
| Wands                      | When targeted by an effect from a magical wand.                                  | Save v. Magic                   | Magical influence                                             |
| Paralysis or Petrification | When targeted by an effect that paralyses or turns to stone.                     | Save v. Hold. <br>Save v. Charm | Mobility hazards and force. <br>Force of will and personality |
| Breath Attacks             | When targeted by the breath of a dragon (or other monster with a breath attack). | Save v. Madness                 | Mind alteration and effects                                   |
| Spells, Rods, or Staves    | When targeted by a baneful spell or an effect from a magical rod or staff.       | Save v. Magic                   | Magical influence                                             |
